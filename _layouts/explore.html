Dome 1 - Greetham

Greetham, is named after our local village, situated a short walk along the brook from the campsite. A Beautiful dome decorated in blues and greys. This dome has the uninterrupted view over the brook and the golf course beyond. Set the furthest down the field and boasting the most private hot tub. This dome kitchen faces west for the evening sunset.
